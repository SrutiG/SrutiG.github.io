About Me

My name is Sruti Guhathakurta and I was born in Tempe, Arizona. Some of my favorite things to do when I'm not coding are exploring Atlanta (especially the food scene), running, and spending time with my friends and family.

My approach to the projects I work on is definitely influenced by my background. I went to eight different schools growing up in different states and countries before I settled down at Georgia Tech in 2014. Because of this I enjoy surrounding myself with a diverse group of people and learning from others.
